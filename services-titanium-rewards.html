Earn Points When You Buy Property

As soon as you make your first investment in residential or commercial real estate with The First Group, you enter into our investors club and gain access to Titanium Rewards. For every future purchase you make with us, you will earn one Titanium Point. Redeem these points for $5,000 in cash or $10,000 of investment credit or choose from one of our amazing partner benefits featuring Jumeirah properties, Wyndham hotels and Etihad Airways.

Earn Points When You refer

Sharing the experience you have with The First Group not only allows a friend, relative or colleague to enjoy the same opportunities, it also rewards you with one Titanium Point for every personal referral who buys rental property with us. All we ask is that you provide their contact information and tell us how you know them and then we will do the rest, while giving them the same VIP treatment that you received.

BRING A BUYER’ AND RECEIVE POINTS PLUS A COMPLIMENTARY DUBAI TRIP

With the “Bring a Buyer” program, simply travel to Dubai with a qualified referral guest and invite them to learn more about our exciting hotel investment opportunities. If they choose to invest with us while on your trip together, you will be rewarded with one Titanium Point as well as a complimentary Dubai trip for two, including flights and hotel accommodation.

Earn Extra Partner Privileges

Through our collaboration with the Jumeirah Group and Emirates Skywards, Titanium Rewards gives you access to even more exciting privileges. Upon earning your first Titanium Point, you will receive a Jumeirah Sirius Gold Card to enjoy top tier privileges at Jumeirah hotels and resorts. You will then receive another year of Gold membership when you achieve three and five Titanium Point levels. With Emirates Skywards partner privileges, you can enjoy Gold tier membership for one year, subject to eligible investment.
